# Build Brief: Glowverse Skincare (RHS) Redesign

This document synthesizes client brand extraction and competitor analysis into a master plan for the **Glowverse** (RHS) redesign.

## Design Direction

### 1. Aesthetic: "Skin Longevity Luxury"
- **Vibe**: Premium, clinical, yet ethereal. Think "Apple meets High-End Beauty."
- **Color Palette**:
    - **Primary**: #FFFFFF (Clean canvas)
    - **Secondary**: #0A0A0C (Deep minimalist darks for footer/headers)
    - **Accent**: #D4E3F0 (Soft "Ice Blue" for glows/highlights)
- **Typography**:
    - **Headings**: `Instrument Serif` (Elegant, authoritative)
    - **Body**: `Inter` (Clean, modern, readable)
- **Visual Effects**:
    - Glassmorphism (`backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`)
    - Subtle grain texture overlay
    - Floating background orbs in accent tones

### 2. The 3D Scroll Hero (SKILL.md)
- **Asset**: A 6-second video of a **Luxury Serum Bottle** deconstructing.
- **Implementation**: Sticky canvas at the top of the page. The bottle pieces separate as the user scrolls down, revealing ingredient "Specs" and "Annotation Cards."

## Site Architecture

### 1. Homepage (The Protocol)
- **Hero**: 3D Scroll-Stop animation with Serum Bottle.
- **Specs**: Count-up animations for "85% Hydration Boost," "24h Barrier Support," etc.
- **Featured Articles**: High-intent guides (Retinol, Skminimalism) organized into the "Radiant Skin Protocol."
- **Social Proof**: Testimonial carousel with authentic UGC placeholders.

### 2. Article Pages
- **Format**: Mode 1 SEO optimized. Long-form, evidence-based content with "Skinimalism" tips.
- **Conversion**: Sidebar newsletter signup ("Join the Protocol").

### 3. About / Mission
- **Tone**: Focus on "Skin Longevity" and "Evidence-Based Rituals."

## Content Framework

- **Primary Headline**: "Elevate Your Skin Longevity Protocol."
- **Secondary Headline**: "Science-backed rituals for radiant, healthy skin."
- **SEO Keywords**: Radiant Healthy Skin (RHS), Skin Longevity, Skinimalism, Barrier Repair.

## Conversion Playbook

- **Goal**: Newsletter signups ("Join the Protocol").
- **Tactic**: Offer a "7-Day Skin Longevity Checklist" as a lead magnet.
- **Trust Signals**: Featured snippet-optimized FAQ sections and "Clinically Proven" badges.
